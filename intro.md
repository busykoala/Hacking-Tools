# Intro

## Topics/Methods

- **Network**  
  unsecure passwords, bad network config, pishing, sql-/xml injection
- **Password**  
  cracking, keylogging, physically
- **Backdoor**  
  unofficial software versions, companies, secret services
- **Virus, Worm, Malware**  
  emails, IoT, ransomware
- **Denial of Service**
